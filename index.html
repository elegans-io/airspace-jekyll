EMAIL MARKETING

Email is still the tool with the highest conversion. In 2016, email generated 174% more conversion than Facebook and Twitter together.

But information overflow is real in email more than anywhere else. Not only Internet Service Providers are very active in filtering senders, but mail clients are also very careful in inserting messages in the inbox --fear the Promotions Tab.

Relevance is key to improve engagement and bring marketing to a higher level

Absolut personalization.
The future of email.

Starting the message with Dear John is not enough. The message must consider how the customer visited the site, where they are. Who they are.

Higher personalization means more information for the user, clean inbox, higher reading and click rate. Which translates into ISP and mail clients recognizing the message as priority.

Reputation. Trust. Relationship.

What is ORAC

ORAC is a echosystem of algorithms, born out of AI systems in health and customer support. Two fields where no mistake is allowed.

NLP

Natural Language Processing. ORAC dimensionality reduction algorithm takes into account language.

History

ORAC analyzes each possible action by the user, extracting preferences --and not just about products.

Effective

ORAC continously runs A/B tests to ensure you, and show to you, its effectiveness.

Smart

ORAC learns by the usage. The more email you send, the more personalized they will be with time.

Use ORAC

Ask your Email Service Provider to enable ORAC placeholders. Using ORAC placeholders you'll be able to persoalize the message sent to each customer. ORAC will even understand if you should greet them with Hallo John! or Dear Mr Smith
